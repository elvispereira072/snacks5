Welcome to Lanchonete Delícias do Lanche

Our History

Lanchonete Delícias do Lanche was founded in 2005 by two friends who were passionate about food and the idea of ​​creating a welcoming space where people could enjoy delicious and succulent snacks.

Since then, we have dedicated ourselves to offering our customers a variety of tasty options, made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Our goal is to provide a memorable dining experience with every visit.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with an exceptional gastronomic experience, offering delicious snacks, refreshing juices and top-quality service. We are committed to promoting the satisfaction and well-being of our customers in a welcoming and relaxed environment.

  • Variety

    Our snack bar offers a wide variety of snacks, from classic burgers to vegetarian and vegan options, to suit all tastes and preferences.

  • Freshness

    We use fresh, carefully selected ingredients in all our preparations, guaranteeing the flavor and quality of our snacks.

  • Flavor

    Our snacks are prepared with great care and dedication, always seeking to provide an explosion of flavors in each bite.

  • Service

    Our team is committed to offering friendly and efficient service, ensuring a pleasant and satisfactory experience for all our customers.
